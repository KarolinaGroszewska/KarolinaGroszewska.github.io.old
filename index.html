Projects

Fingertip
SwiftUI and XCTest. Fingertip is an iOS and watchOS passion project which allows for accountable habit tracking with time-blocking, analytics-based updates, and connection-based accountability.
Atendi
React Native. Atendi is a mobile application I have begun developing with my friends for StudioV, a women's workout community. The goal of the application is to improve attendance tracking by sending location-based push notifications to registered members.
SpotifyMatched
React, Flask, PostgreSQL. Web application allowing Vanguard crew to find other users with similar music tastes. Uses the Spotify API to find similar artists and tracks and calculates a match percentage. I spearheaded the development of the React frontend and work extensively on testing and connections to the Flask backend.
Baby Bear Summer Camp
Unity. A strategic world builder and management simulator centered around creating a welcoming camp for young bears, created by V:Pog during our first year as an organization.
Soba
React, MongoDB. Social platform that attempts to match students with dormitories based on their interests and currently considered roommates. Uses current user-generated housing statistics to determine whether students have access to a housing option through a percentage based-system, shows the amenities, and posts current and past resident reviews. This project won second-place in the full-stack development category at UA Innovate.
SAC Sign-In
Swift, Firebase. Created for the Sexual Assault Center in Nashville, this iPad-native application logs incoming patient information, and then stores that information in secure servers for the staff to access. Started as a part of the Vanderbilt University's Summer VandyHacks 2020.
Brewed Awakening
Swift, CoreData. A point-of-sales system for my high school's coffee shop, which is run by the Career and Life Skills department for students with disabilities. A more disability friendly version of Square with larger display boxes, displaying exact change in cash with images of coins and bank notes, and management tools to facilitate the baristas' and other staff interactions with the app.
